Pick and Place Robot

This project was completed as part of my Master's studies at RPTU Kaiserslautern during the "Master Project Lab" course. We were a group of four students who collaborated on the project and divided responsibilities among us. we designed and programmed a robotic arm for sorting objects based on shape recognition using a camera. The system identifies defective vs. good parts and controls the robot to place them in separate bins. Control logic was implemented using MATLAB and Arduino.

Problem Solving in Industry

Emergency Fix for Olive Oil Filling Machine

In a factory with a production line for filling olive oil bottles, a critical problem arose where bottles were being filled unevenly due to conveyor movement affecting pressure-based fill levels. During a major order, I modified the PLC control panel to stop the conveyor during the filling process and added a VFD (Variable Frequency Drive) to allow the user to adjust filling speed. This solution enabled the factory to complete the order on time without losses.

Restoring Automated Stove in a Sweets Factory

An industrial stove failed to start just after large dough batches were prepared. I diagnosed and solved three critical issues: a faulty door sensor, a damaged relay, and incorrect wiring caused by user intervention. The stove was restored in time, preventing significant product loss and maintaining production continuity.
